What to wear?

    We’re hoping for warm, sunny weather, but please come prepared for chilly evenings. If you’re biking to the park, wear something comfortable — the rental bikes are Dutch-style, so dresses are doable! You’re also welcome to bring a change of clothes for the park. A few vehicles will be heading there and can carry extra jackets, shoes, bags, etc.
