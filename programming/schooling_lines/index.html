Schooling Lines


This artwork can be viewed in 3D using the cross-eyed method of stereoscopic free-viewing.

Instructions:
Keeping the screen at arms length, relax your eyes until the two images overlap. Allow a few minutes for your eyes to adjust properly. Following the movement of a single line can help them focus.

It is important to keep the screen rotationally aligned with your eyes. Slowly rotate your head (or computer) from side to side until the images "snap" together.

When viewed successfully a crisp image with stereoscopic depth should be visible. Prolonged viewing is not recommended.
