Schooling Lines


This artwork can be viewed using the cross-eyed method of stereoscopic free-viewing described below.

Keeping the screen at arms length, allow your eyes to relax until three white circles appear. Train your gaze on the middle circle for a few minutes while your eyes refocus. Following the movement of a single line can help speed this process.

It is important to keep the screen rotationally aligned with your eyes. Slowly rotate your head (or computer) from side to side until the circles "snap" together.

It may be helpful to position an object with a circular opening halfway between the screen and the eyes so that the left circle is only visible from the right eye and the right circle is only visible from the left eye.
