Ghosts / Matthis Grunsky


Ghosts

MFA Thesis Exhibition - Emily Carr University

March 2016 - Concourse Gallery, Vancouver, British Columbia


Ghosts featured nine white monochrome paintings and a series of plastic water bottles cast in plaster.
Three rectangular holes are cut from each plywood panel, which are mounted to the walls and painted with the gallery's standard wall paint. A yellow plywood tabletop supports the contorted plaster casts in the center of the space.
