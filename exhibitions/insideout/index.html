Inside Out

Collaborative Exhibition with M.E. Sparks
June 16-19, 2018 @SiteFactory
1600 Block Franklin St. Vancouver, British Columbia

Looking at artist studios as a social and historical trope, Inside Out explored the nature of these spaces within the contemporary moment of increasingly dematerialized art practices and high rental costs in Vancouver.
Hosted within the SiteFactory bus, the exhibition featured a set of found and constructed objects referencing historical depictions of the artist's studio. A series of algorithmically animated drawings were projected on the white objects in an attempt to create a "living" painting within the space. Visitors to the exhibition were encouraged to enter into the projection and become part of the "painting".
