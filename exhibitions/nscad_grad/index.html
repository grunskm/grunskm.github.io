Matthis Grunsky - Paintings

BFA Graduation Exhibition - NSCAD University

October 23-30, 2012, Gallery 1, Anna Leonowens Gallery, Halifax, NS

This solo exhibition of paintings occurred during the year of my graduation from NSCAD University. It featured paintings from the Sidewalk series that depict patterns of chewing gum stuck on city sidewalks.

These paintings feature the use of a glazing technique in which multiple sidewalk images are layered in sequence. A tinted glaze is applied between each layer of dots, slowly obscuring earlier marks and generating a deep visual depth.
