Cursor Echo

Cursor Echo is an interactive livestreamed preformance/website in which visitors' mouse movements are temporarily recorded through video feedback. The distortion and compression of livestreamed video causes the recorded gestures to degrade over time, leading to an atmospheric and disorienting experience.
