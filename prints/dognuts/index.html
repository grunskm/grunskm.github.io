Dognuts


The Dognuts are an infinite algorithmic series of PNG images, each measuring 900x900 pixels. They are generated using a numerical seed which determines the form of each image. Dognuts may be displayed on a computer screen at native resolution, or they may be scaled and printed using any printing technique.

For acquisition information, please inquire at grunskm@gmail.com
