Bio

I am a visual artist, post-secondary instructor, and media lab technician focused on creative coding, digital fabrication, and studio-based teaching. I have developed many original university courses and community workshops that connect drawing, painting, mathematics, and code, and maintain an active studio practice in Winnipeg, MB.

Highlights

  • Extensive experience as a technician in digital media labs at Emily Carr University and the University of Winnipeg.
  • 20+ sections taught at the University of Manitoba since Fall 2021.
  • Developed 5 post-secondary courses: Painting Systems, Perspectives on Perspective, Coding Sketchbook, Sketching Time, and Painting Through the Screen.
  • Developed original workshops in creative coding and digital literacy.
  • Awarded two Canada Council for the Arts Research and Creation grants in 2020 and 2022 ($25,000 each).
