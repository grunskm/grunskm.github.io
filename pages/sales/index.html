As of January 2020 I will no longer accept money in exchange for my artwork.

The primary reason for this is because I would like my work to be accessible to anyone who wants it. Even at its cheapest, purchasing art is unaffordable for most people, including myself. To me this is unconscionable. I would rather make zero profit from my work, than know that it is only accessible to those with money.

If you are interested in obtaining an artwork that you see on this website, please send me a message specifying which piece you would like. Provided the work is available, you may have it for the cost of shipping.

I do accept monetary and material contributions from those who wish to support my studio rent and material costs.
