MATTHIS GRUNSKY

I use computational systems to produce drawings and paintings that explore the aesthetics of computer code, the natural world, and our propensity for finding beauty in chaotic information.
I'm currently making drawings using a pen plotter of my own design and experimenting with browser-based stereoscopic animation.

As of July 2021, I am living on Treaty One Territoy in Winnipeg, MB and teaching at the University of Manitoba. I was previously based in Vancouver BC, and am originally from Halifax NS.

Get in touch with me at grunskm@gmail.com if you want to chat about drawing with code, pen plotting, painting, stereoscopy, (or anything else really).
