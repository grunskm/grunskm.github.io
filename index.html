Hi! 🍳 I am an artist living in Vancouver, Canada. I work with code to make drawings and paintings that explore the aesthetics of computation and the intersections of physical and digital materials.

I'm currently delving into the world of machine drawing using my homemade pen plotter and experimenting with procedurally animated stereoscopic drawings.

Get in touch with me at grunskm@gmail.com if you want to chat about creative coding, pen plotting, painting, stereoscopy, (or anything else really).

Exhibition @ Trapp Projects

I will be having an exhibition of recent work at Trapp Projects in Vancouver between June 12 and June 25. If you're in the area please drop by on weekends between 12-5PM. The work will include a series of drawings produced with my homemade pen plotter and a large stereoscopic diptych!

To-Do-To-Do

Presented by Number 3 Gallery as part of their SPAM monthly newsletter, To-Do-To-Do is a collaboration between M.E. Sparks and myself. The project features a series of smaller web pages looking at different forms of list making.
