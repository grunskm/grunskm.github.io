Matthis Grunsky
paper stereo paint browser

Produced for the Prairie Art Book Fair in 2024, this stereo card series was designed for cross-eyed free-viewing (no viewer required), and sold in pre-sealed mixed packs of ten. Each image pair was generated algorithmically and printed with a pen plotter, carefully mimicking the gesture of a signature, with broad swirls and tight, knotted sections. Five example packs are included below as a representative grouping of the larger, open series.
