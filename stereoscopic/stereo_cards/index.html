Matthis Grunsky
paper stereo paint browser

Made for the Prairie Art Book Fair in 2024, this stereo card series was produced in packs of six for cross-eyed free-viewing (no viewer required). Each image pair was generated algorithmically and printed with a pen plotter using a custom drawing system that simulates the gesture of a signature, balancing broad swirls with sharper, tighter marks to create depth through binocular alignment.
